Cayuga Weightlifting Club

The Cayuga Weightlifting Club is a premier facility located upstate in Ithaca, New York. We have variety of training programs, equipment, and a trained instructor to help you achieve any goals you might have. We are open to lifters of all levels – from those with no experience to those who might be looking to compete nationally. Come visit us and learn more about how to get started.
